About Us

Meet the Family Behind Graze and Grow

Our Story

We’re a husband and wife team raising two young kids and a small herd of happy horses in beautiful Woodinville, WA. Our passion for the outdoors, healthy living, good nutrition, and sustainable practices led us to create Graze and Grow.

We believe that the best gardens and strongest communities are built on healthy soil, responsible stewardship, and a love of nature. Our family spends as much time outside as possible—tending to our animals, growing our own food, and exploring the trails and parks in our area.

Why We Do This

As lifelong horse lovers and avid gardeners, we wanted to find a way to give back to our land and our neighbors. Turning clean, well-managed horse manure into rich, organic compost was a natural solution—one that benefits our horses, our gardens, and our community.

We’re proud to offer high-quality compost that helps other families and gardeners grow healthy, vibrant plants—naturally and sustainably.

What Matters to Us

  • Family, community, and connection to the outdoors
  • Happy, healthy horses and ethical animal care
  • Quality nutrition—for people, animals, and plants
  • Giving back to the land and supporting local gardens
  • Simple, honest practices and products you can trust

Thank You

Thank you for supporting our small family business. We love meeting new neighbors, sharing tips, and helping your gardens thrive. If you see us at the barn, on the trails, or out delivering compost—say hello!
