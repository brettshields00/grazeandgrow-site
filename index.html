Welcome to Graze and Grow

Graze and Grow is dedicated to providing premium, organic horse manure compost for gardens and landscapes in the Woodinville area. Our mission is to help your soil and plants thrive through natural, sustainable practices—benefitting your garden, your community, and the planet.

The Benefits of Organic Compost

Using organic compost in your garden:

  • Improves soil fertility and nutrient content
  • Increases water retention and reduces irrigation needs
  • Boosts plant health, yield, and resilience
  • Reduces dependence on chemical fertilizers
  • Supports beneficial soil microbes and earthworms
  • Reduces landfill waste and greenhouse gas emissions

Compost is a natural way to enhance your garden’s productivity and support a healthy ecosystem.
