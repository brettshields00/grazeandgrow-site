About Our Compost

We produce high-quality compost made from horse manure right here in Woodinville, WA. Our compost is perfect for enriching soil, retaining moisture, and improving your garden's vitality.

Why Compost Matters

Healthy soil is the foundation of every garden. Our compost feeds the soil naturally and helps reduce dependence on chemical fertilizers.
