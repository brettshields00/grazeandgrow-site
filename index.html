About Our Compost

At Graze and Grow, we are passionate about sustainable gardening and healthy soil. Our compost is made using only natural horse manure and bedding, aged and turned regularly to encourage beneficial microbes and rich organic matter.

  • Produced locally in Woodinville, WA
  • Screened and aged for maximum nutrient content
  • Free of persistent herbicides and chemical additives
  • Safe for vegetable gardens, lawns, flower beds, and landscaping

Why Compost Matters

Healthy soil is the foundation of every thriving garden. Our premium compost offers multiple benefits:

  • Boosts soil structure and water retention
  • Provides slow-release nutrients for plants
  • Supports beneficial soil organisms
  • Reduces landfill waste and supports local agriculture

Whether you’re an experienced gardener or just starting out, adding compost improves your soil health naturally and sustainably—no synthetic fertilizers needed.
